For My Princess Elisa 👑

Happy Birthday, my love. This page was made especially for you. ❤️

"A letter written with love, for the most precious gift God has ever given me — Elisa." ✨

All'amore della mia vita,

Buon compleanno, il mio cuore 💘

As I write these words late at night, trying to think of how to make a letter for you without you immediately knowing it was me, I find myself feeling grateful all over again.

Every day since I met you, I have thanked God for the wonderful gift He sent into my life: you.

To be honest, a few months ago I was praying to Saint Xenia of Saint Petersburg. She is known for helping people find a spouse, and I truly believe she heard my prayers. How blessed am I that God listened to her intercessions and answered them in such a beautiful way.

Your kindness, which showed me your care every day, slowly opened my heart. 😄

Your mesmerizing green eyes looking at me with so much love. 👀

Your fabulous smile whenever I make you laugh or giggle. 🙈

Your lovely and contagious laugh that melts me like a candle. 🫠

Your funny silliness that makes me laugh so hard. 😆

The way you care for me and make me feel deeply loved. 😍

The love you give so freely and so sincerely. 🥰

That adorable look you give me when you're angry or hungry, which is somehow still the cutest thing ever. 😋

The joy you bring into my life. 🤗

The hugs and kisses you give from the bottom of your heart. 😩

Your patience with me whenever I struggle to explain myself clearly. 🥹

And not only with me, but with everyone around you. You share the love from your sweet heart with others so naturally. Even after everything you've been through, you have kept your loving heart in a world where so many people turn away from Christ. I am so proud of you for that.

I still remember when we first met on Sky.

It felt like pure luck to find such a loving soul in a world like that.

But as people say, God knows exactly when the time is right.

I trusted Him, and He gave me not only what I wanted, but what I truly needed in my life:

My Elisa.

We both know we met through a game, and we live hours apart by plane. Sometimes it is difficult because we cannot see each other whenever we want.

Yet somehow, our story proves that true love can be found in unexpected places and can grow stronger even when two people are far apart.

All it needs is dedication from both hearts.

And I truly believe that, under God's providence, we will make it.

I also remember when you were troubled by something and thought I would never forgive you.

When you opened your heart to me, it showed me so much.

It made me feel even closer to you.

It showed me how much trust you placed in me and how every day you try to show me how deeply you love me.

My sweet Elisa, I never imagined we would reach the place we are today.

Every time you smile behind your phone, it feels like a victory for me.

Not because I am obligated to make you smile, but because seeing my Elisa smile is one of the most beautiful things in the world.

That simple smile brightens my entire day.

And your laugh... and your voice.

You always say your voice is annoying or ugly.

I know it isn't.

And one day, little by little, I will prove it to you.

Hearing your voice was one of the best days I have ever had.

Your happiness is my happiness.

Your sadness is my sadness.

If you are bothered, overthinking, anxious, or carrying any of those awful burdens, I want to help make them disappear.

Because a soul like yours was never meant to carry them alone.

You have a heart overflowing with love and care for everyone around you.

As our God is full of love, so are you.

You were, you are, and you always will be.

As we celebrate your birthday today, my prayer is simple:

May God continue to bless you.

May He protect your beautiful heart.

May He guide your steps.

May He fill your life with joy, peace, and countless reasons to smile.

And may He always remind you how deeply you are loved.

I hope we continue growing together just as we are now.

I want to build a future with you, amore mio.

And I will love you to the moon and back. 💞


Ti amo, Elisa. Oggi, domani e per sempre. ❤️


Forever yours,

Christopher ❤️
